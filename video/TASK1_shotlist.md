# "Observation" longform video

## Shotlist v0.2

01
You will see conflict, bad faith, no faith, and more.
You will also see kindness, goodwill and good nature;
And silliness (galore).

02
Real dynamics exist and real pain too. 
Negative energy is everywhere: pervasive, relentless;
Everyone losing their cool.

03
Most live in fear and in shadows;
We're all lonely and tired.
Mired in debt; withdrawn and addicted; so many liars.

04
This is reality, our world circa now.
But that's not all there is, thank god:
There's hope, there's promise, not all of it's foul.

05
There's love. There's care. There's focus and drive. 
New marvels arrive daily: new knowledge and expression;
New tech and techniques thrive.

06
New loves; new friends; new ways to improve.
We're decent and loving; we're happy and weird. 
We just want good times; to maybe eat some good food.

07
To love and be loved. To laugh and be silly. To share with our kind.
To share and see. To be seen and heard. (Not me!)
To forget the great grind.

08
But still no one listens or cares. Or is that just you?
Oh no, not me. It's them, not me.
Over and over again: the angst renewed.

09
The game never ends; no mercy, no quarter; cheered on by our clowns.
The clock never tolls. (Who could hear it regardless behind noise of the trolls?)
They're out in the open, the show goes on. And our curtains go down.

10
Alone again and hungry. What do I eat? What show do I watch? I just need some good luck.
Who did what? I heard this. No you're wrong and I'm right.
They're no good; I know best; I don't give a fuck.

11
These are your people. This is their chaos. Too much nuance to fathom.
Lives and souls. Families and pets. Desires and stresses.
Who are all these people? A king knows not of their kingdom.

12
But good change is possible. We're all in this together.
If we share a little compassion: care and attention;
Think first of kindness and good nature.

13
So be here for peace. Be an arbiter of good.
Diffuse situations; take care always; 
Always be (in a) good (mood).

14
Be a light in a cave; a mirage in a desert; a breeze of fresh air.
A romantic in love. An honest beggar among thieves. Be the best of who you are.
I trust you and know you'll be the one who shows care.

15
This is your task. That is all there is: Be good and true and kind.
Let's lighten the load, lower the temp.
Our shared common ground find.

16
Thanks for your time and your skill. I'm here to help too.
Thanks for your your scale and best effort.
You have nothing to prove.

17
I'm looking forward to working alongside.
Lets learn and get better. Let's figure it out.
We're together, you and me, we're along for the ride.

18
Take your time and think deeply; always consider the best how.
We're in no rush. We're in no hurry.
But no time like the now!
