## "Observation" longform video

**"XYZ"**

The above is your scene/shot. More context below.

### Core instructions

1. Our work is more akin to a music video than a documentary. 
2. All frames should be moody, dramatic, minimal, and ***cool***.
3. Use vibrant colors or deep/crushed black + whites; or a combo of both could be cool too 😎
4. But always noir and edgy. With a core humor and wry wit.
5. Symbolism is great and expected. 
6. Let loose and have fun.
7. At end, all frames must be *beautiful* — use well composed, abstract shots and scenes of maximum loveliness + creativity.
8. Focus on *Nature — focus on plants and materials; the awe of the natural world; keep humans and animals to a minimum unless the are truly needed.*
9. There must be clear + direct activity in the frame.

### Camera

1. Fast framerate IMAX camera with long 480mm lens.
2. Camera movement pushes in to the center of frame in mix of high speed + ultra slow motion ("ramping")
3. The only camera movement is direct, straight push/dolly forward.
